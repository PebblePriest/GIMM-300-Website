Reid Daniels

My name is Reid Daniels. I am a Sophmore at Boise State University while having a year at College of Western Idaho as well. I am a member of the GIMM program, looking to better my coding experience and become a video game programer in the future. I have lived in Idaho most of my life, so the idea of working in another state excites me greatly.

Experience

Over the years I have been able to code in multiple languages. Not including the obvious with websites, I have learned how to code JavaScript, C#, ActionScript, and Python. Though I may not be an expert at these, I have used them many times for multiple projects. I have also dabbled in website creation, making a few over the years, as well as using Unity to make small games as well as multiple adobe projects. I am also certified in Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, and Access.
