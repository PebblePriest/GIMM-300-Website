Scurvy Wars

Fight off a horde of octopus pirates and protect your brothers from being eaten!

My first ever game!

My goal throughout all these years of school was to make a game. My first semester of college, that goal was achieved. Even if the game is a simple side by side shooter, it gave me the idea of how games are made and the work and thought that have to go into it. It opened my eyes to the future, and what I was actually trying to do. Because of this game, I am where I am now in the program. It is not perfect, and it still has some errors in it, but I love it all the same. Here is a trailer to show off the key points of gameplay!(Need Flash to play!)
